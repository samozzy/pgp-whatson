How to Become a Taiwanese: A Musical

National Taiwan Normal University Graduated Institute of Performing Arts

The Studio
Paradise in Augustines

Musicals and Opera

Performances: 21st, 22nd, 23rd, 24th August
Start time: 10:00 (1hr)

Taiwan is a country located in Asia, and many people say that the most beautiful scenery in Taiwan is its people. What does it take to be a true Taiwanese? How can one become a Taiwanese? Despite living in Taiwan for many years, we seem unable to define and prove ourselves. Therefore, we intend to trace back to our growth experience, to explore the clues that can prove ourselves as Taiwanese. Let's return to the most authentic Taiwanese life and experience it together!
