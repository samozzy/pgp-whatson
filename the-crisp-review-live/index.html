Comedian and crisp connoisseur Adam Evans presents The Crisp Review: Live, nominated for Best Theatre and Best Newcomer at the Greater Manchester Fringe 2022! The show documents the rise, fall and rise again of one of lockdown's most lingering cultural movements. With mixed-media comedy, a showcase of reviews old and new and – of course – free gourmet crisps for every audience member!
